This file gives guidelines to make connections for the main program

I2C LCD display module 
-> GND --- groung
-> VCC --- +5 volt
-> SDA --- A4
-> SCL --- A5

2 axis Joystick module
-> GND --- ground
-> VCC --- +5 volt
-> VRx --- A0
-> VRy --- A1
-> SW  --- A2

IR reciever
-> GND --- ground
-> VCC --- +5 volt
-> sensor out --- D2

IR diode
-> D8
